How it helps

Swimming is a fantastic full-body workoutthat engages numerous muscle groups,
making it ideal for building strength and endurance without putting excessive stress on growing bodies.
It's a gentle exercise that can also improve cardiovascular health and flexibility.

    Fun Facts!

  • Studies show that swimming can reduce exercise-induced asthma.
  • Over half of the American population can't swim!
